    Charcuterie Boards

    These beautiful, handcrafted charcuterie boards are made out of a combination of Ash, Purple heart, maple and cherry providing a stunning rustic yet contemporary display option for any assortment of meats & cheeses.

    Seasonal Pre-made gifts

    Slate

    This beautiful, handcrafted charcuterie board provides a stunning rustic yet contemporary display option for any assortment of meats & cheeses. Handmade in our home workshop, these make the perfect gift for housewarmings, weddings, anniversaries, or gift to yourself. Each board is allowed to acclimate prior to selection process where we select the boards with the most unique grain characteristics. Each Board is finished with our very own food grade butcher block wax, giving the board a softer touch.
